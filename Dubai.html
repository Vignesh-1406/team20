DUBAI
The Jewel of the Desert

Discover Dubai’s iconic skyline, luxurious experiences, and cultural treasures.

Discover Dubai

Dubai is a dazzling metropolis in the UAE known for its ultramodern architecture, luxury shopping, and vibrant nightlife. From the world’s tallest building, Burj Khalifa, to the traditional souks of Old Dubai, this city offers a dynamic fusion of old and new. Relax on pristine beaches, go dune bashing in the desert, or indulge in world-class dining. Dubai is where adventure, opulence, and culture come together in spectacular fashion.

Top Destinations

Burj Khalifa

Marvel at the tallest building in the world and enjoy panoramic city views.

Palm Jumeirah

Man-made island shaped like a palm, home to luxury hotels and beaches.

Dubai Mall

One of the largest shopping malls globally, offering shopping, dining, and entertainment.

Dubai Miracle Garden

A stunning floral display showcasing various themed gardens.

Dubai Frame

See old and new Dubai from a towering picture frame-style structure.

Global Village

A multicultural theme park with food, shopping, and entertainment from around the world.

Festivals of Dubai

Dubai Shopping Festival

A month-long retail celebration with entertainment, discounts, and fireworks.

Untold Dubai Festival

A music and electronic dance music festival held annually in Dubai.

UAE National Day

Celebrate the spirit of the UAE with parades, fireworks, and cultural showcases.

Adventure Activities

Desert Safari

Go dune bashing, enjoy camel rides, and experience a desert sunset dinner.

Skydiving

Skydive over the Palm Jumeirah for the ultimate thrill and view.

Dubai Aquarium

Walk through a massive aquarium tunnel filled with marine life in Dubai Mall.

Special Foods

Shawarma

A popular Middle Eastern wrap filled with spiced meat and garlic sauce.

Machboos

A traditional Emirati rice dish cooked with spices and meat or seafood.

Luqaimat

Sweet fried dumplings drizzled with date syrup – a Ramadan favorite.

Harees

A creamy wheat and meat dish often enjoyed during festivals.

Mutabbaq

A stuffed savory pancake popular across the Gulf.

Karak Tea

A strong spiced tea served across cafes and roadside stalls in Dubai.

Hotels & Stays

Atlantis The Palm

Luxury resort with underwater suites and a water park at Palm Jumeirah.

Burj Al Arab

The world’s only 7-star hotel, famous for its sail-shaped silhouette and opulence.

Budget Hostels

Clean and affordable accommodations for backpackers and solo travelers.

Must Buys

Gold Jewelry

Dubai’s famous gold souks offer high-quality, intricately designed gold jewelry.

Persian Rugs

Handwoven rugs with unique patterns and vibrant colors, perfect for home decor.

Arabic Perfumes

Exotic perfumes made from oud, rose, and musk, available in luxurious bottles.

Transportation Guide

Getting around Dubai is seamless and modern:

    Airports: Dubai International Airport (DXB), Al Maktoum International

    Metro: Automated metro lines connecting major city areas

    Road: Taxis, trams, and buses are widely available, with ride-sharing options
